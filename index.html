About Trouver AI

Our mission is to harness the power of open-source AI to solve Africa's most pressing challenges and create sustainable growth across the continent.

We believe that artificial intelligence, when developed openly and collaboratively, can drive innovation and address unique African challenges in ways that proprietary solutions cannot.
